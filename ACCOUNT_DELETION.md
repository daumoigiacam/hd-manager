# Account And Data Deletion Guide

Google Play va App Store thuong yeu cau app co cach de nguoi dung yeu cau xoa tai khoan/du lieu.

## De xoa tai khoan nhan su hoac khach hang

1. Nguoi dung gui yeu cau toi chu doanh nghiep hoac bo phan quan tri.
2. Quan tri vien kiem tra so dien thoai/tai khoan.
3. Quan tri vien khoa tai khoan truoc khi xoa neu can doi soat.
4. Xoa hoac an danh hoa cac ban ghi lien quan theo quy dinh cua cong ty.

## Du lieu co the can giu lai

Mot so du lieu ke toan, hoa don, giao dich thanh toan, nhat ky hoat dong co the can duoc giu lai theo yeu cau phap ly/doi soat.
